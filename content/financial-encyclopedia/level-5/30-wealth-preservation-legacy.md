# Chapter 30: Wealth Preservation & Legacy

## 30.1 Multi-Generational Wealth Planning
This coordinates investments and estate structures so wealth supports multiple generations. Core pillars include:
* **Written Family Vision:** Defining money values.
* **Family Governance:** Regular family meetings to educate heirs on stewardship.

## 30.2 Asset Protection
Using irrevocable trusts and specific legal entities to shield assets from creditors and litigation risks.

# Chapter 24: International Finance

## 24.1 Currency Exchange (Forex)
The price of one currency expressed in terms of another (e.g., EUR/USD). [cite_start]Rates are influenced by interest rates, economic growth, and geopolitical stability[cite: 24].

## 24.2 Investing in Foreign Markets
* [cite_start]**ADRs (American Depositary Receipts)**: Certificates representing shares in a foreign stock, traded on U.S. exchanges[cite: 24].
* [cite_start]**Emerging Markets**: Investing in faster-growing but higher-risk economies like India, Brazil, or Southeast Asia[cite: 24].

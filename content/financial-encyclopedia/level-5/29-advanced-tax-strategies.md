# Chapter 29: Advanced Tax Strategies

## 29.1 Trust Taxation
Trusts are separate tax entities. They are generally categorized as:
* **Grantor Trusts:** Income is taxed to the creator.
* **Non-Grantor Trusts:** Taxed as a separate entity or to the beneficiaries.

## 29.2 Real Estate & Planning Tools
* **1031 Exchanges:** Deferring capital gains taxes by reinvesting property sale proceeds into "like-kind" real estate.
* **Charitable Remainder Trusts (CRT):** Providing income to the donor while ensuring the remainder goes to charity, offering immediate tax deductions.
